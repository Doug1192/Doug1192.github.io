About Me

Master of Science in Financial Technology Analytics

Hello! 👋

I'm Doug Chingosho, a graduate student pursuing an MS in Business Analytics with a concentration in Financial Technology Analytics at Washington University in St. Louis, Olin Business School. I'm passionate about leveraging data science and quantitative analysis to solve complex financial problems and drive strategic decision-making.

With over 5 years of experience in financial services, I've developed a strong foundation in risk management, data administration, and team leadership. My journey from managing pension fund assets to pursuing advanced analytics reflects my commitment to continuous learning and excellence.
